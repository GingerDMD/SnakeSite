Dimethylhexylamine

AKA: DMHA, 2-aminoisoheptane

Background

DMHA was introduced into preworkout products as a novel stimulant once the FDA started cracking down on products containing DMAA. The two compounds are structurally very similar, differing only in the length of the alkyl chain (DMHA is one carbon longer) and in the position of the methyl group. DMHA is purported by many sites to be about 80% as strong as DMAA (when compared mg for mg), although many people say it simply doesn't feel the same.

In my experience, DMHA does feel very much like DMAA, which structurally makes sense. I would even go so far as to say that DMHA is superior to DMAA in many aspects; the crash seems lighter, it doesn't build tolerance nearly as quickly, and it simply feels a bit more "functional". I do think that DMHA needs to be consumed in larger amounts to get effects comparable to DMAA, with ideal effects occurring in the 120 - 200 mg range. Two of my favorite preworkouts of all time, ConvictSTIM and AAN Extreme DMHA, contain the compound in sufficient quantity.



DMHA is somewhat ineffective without caffeine, but synergizes excellently with the compound. It adds a lot of focus and "mental energy", which consequently makes it easier to tune out everything else when exercising and only focus on your movements.
